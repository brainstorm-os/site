The release that reaches out. Mail is no longer IMAP-only: connect over
**Gmail**, **Microsoft 365**, or **JMAP** (Fastmail) for full two-way send and
sync — add an account, sign in, and your inbox works both ways.

Your automations reach the outside world too. A workflow can now fire on an
**incoming webhook** — point a form, a service, or a script at its URL — or
when a **watched file changes** on disk, or once each time the app **starts up**.

Plus: creating a vault in **Downloads** or a **OneDrive** folder on Windows no
longer trips over hidden housekeeping files, the emoji picker's number keycaps
and ©/® render again, and every glance widget's toolbar lines up on one baseline.
