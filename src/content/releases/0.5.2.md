The one where Brainstorm stops being pink. People told us the Rose light theme
was a lot — so the defaults moved: fresh vaults now open in **Default Light**
(neutral surfaces, a violet-indigo accent) and switch into **Default Dark**.
The app icon, welcome screen, and default wallpaper — a new low-poly
green-valley scene — all follow the same indigo identity you see on this site.

Nothing is taken away: Rose and Midnight are still one click away in
Settings → Appearance, and if you already picked a theme, your vault keeps it.

There's also a round of Settings polish (disabled buttons you can still read,
steadier app lists, a cleaner loading spinner) riding along.

macOS builds are signed with a Developer ID and notarized by Apple. Windows is
currently unsigned (SmartScreen: **More info → Run anyway**). Existing installs
update in-app from **Settings → Updates**.

It's still a beta: keep backups of anything important. Your vault is a plain
folder you fully control.
