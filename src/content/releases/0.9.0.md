Work with the agent. Ask in chat and it drafts real work — **notes**, **tasks**,
**events**, **bookmarks**, and **contacts** — as cards you review. Edit a field,
approve what's right, discard the rest. Nothing lands in your vault until you say
so: even a hidden instruction buried in a document can only ever draft a card,
never save one. The approve button is yours.

Your team can work on one vault without a server in the middle. **LAN sync** lets
two devices on the same network share a vault directly, peer to peer.

Brainstorm now speaks your language too — the shell and every built-in app are
translated into **French**, **German**, **Italian**, and **Portuguese**, joining
English and Spanish.

Plus: files you **download** in the built-in browser save straight into your
vault, **Notion** pages import as clean Markdown notes, a saved **form** can
become a type's default layout, and a batch of fixes across Calendar, the
dashboard, and the editor.
