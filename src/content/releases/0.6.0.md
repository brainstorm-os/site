The one that gives you your whole mailbox back. The sync window you chose at
connect is now just a starting point: **Load older mail** at the bottom of
the list walks every folder deeper into history, and your position is
remembered — each press resumes exactly where the last one stopped, all the
way back to your oldest message. Big mailboxes render in pages, so nothing
slows down along the way.

And when a mail server rejects your password, fixing it no longer means
starting over: **Reconnect** opens pre-filled with the account's stored
settings — type the new password and everything you'd already synced is
still there. The sidebar's account menu gains **Edit connection** for
changing hosts or the sync window any time.
